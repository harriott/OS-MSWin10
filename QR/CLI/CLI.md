#### pane contents

##### scrolls

#### panes
- `alt+D` duplicate pane right
- `alt+_` split pane below (AZERTY `alt+6`)
- `alt++` split pane right
- `alt+arrow` move focus
- `alt+shift+-` = `split down`
- `alt+shift+arrow` = adjusts pane size
- `ctrl+shift+w` close pane
